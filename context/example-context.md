## Company
Acme Fictional GmbH, 850 employees, regulated financial services, headquartered in Frankfurt. Three business units: payments (core revenue), lending software, advisory. IT is centralized; each business unit has its own product organization.

## Decision-making reality
- The CISO, the works council, and internal audit review anything that touches customer data. Plan for all three from day one, not as a final gate.
- Budget decisions above 50k need two managing directors. Below that, one sponsor suffices.
- The organization is reactive by habit. Proposals that require sustained proactive effort need an explicitly named owner or they stall.

## Current AI status
- Company-wide AI policy published in Q1, aligned with the EU AI Act
- One internal LLM platform live with ~200 users, access-governed
- Two proof-of-concept pilots running (document search, support drafting), nothing in production yet

## Language and tone
- Internal documents: German, formal "Sie" with external parties, first names internally
- The user writes without hype vocabulary. Avoid "revolutionary", "game-changing", superlatives.
